01 : About me

Programmer

I have spent the last 4 years immersed in the world of programming, particularly Front-End Web Development. I've always had a love for computers and the internet, starting at a young age tinkering around with game mods and VFX YouTube videos. These days I spend my time freelancing - helping businesses and individuals realize their goals of having a beautiful modern website.

Musician

My other true love is that of music. I've been a pianist for over two decades and have been working as a professional musician on the side for two years. It's a creative endeavor - a way to express yourself beyond what is possible with words alone.

A Romantic in the Modern Era

I'm a person who savors the beauty of life. Be it the majesty of a sunset, the crisp morning air in a mountain forest, the still serenity of a babbling brook. I believe in the rejuvenating power of nature, and the potential for humanity to be a force for good. In our modern world, the internet gives us the ability to see the best of what humanity has to offer. In an instant we can watch a livestream of an astronaut in Null G on the ISS or talk to a person on the other side of the globe - to find that we're not so different after all. That's a wonderful thing, and I'm grateful to be able to share in it.
